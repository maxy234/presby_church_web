About Our Church

Discover our rich history, mission, and the people who make our church family special

Who We Are

The Presbyterian Church of Ghana is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to worshiping God, sharing the love of Christ, and serving our community. Founded on biblical principles and rooted in the Reformed tradition, we strive to be a light in our community and beyond.

Our church family is diverse, welcoming people from all walks of life to experience God's love and grow in their faith journey. We believe in the power of community, the importance of discipleship, and the call to serve others as Christ served us.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, witness, service, and fellowship, to the glory of God.

Our Vision

A transformed community where every person experiences God's love and lives out their faith in daily life.

Our Core Values

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our guide for faith and practice.

Loving Community

We foster a welcoming environment where everyone can experience genuine fellowship.

Servant Leadership

We follow Christ's example of serving others with humility and compassion.

Cultural Relevance

We engage our Ghanaian heritage while remaining faithful to the gospel.

Our Journey Through Time

1828

The Beginning

The Presbyterian Church of Ghana was established by the Basel Missionaries at Osu, Accra.

1918

Growth & Expansion

The church expanded throughout the Gold Coast, establishing schools and medical facilities.

1957

Post-Independence

Following Ghana's independence, the church continued to grow and impact the nation.

2024

Today

Now one of the largest Protestant denominations in Ghana, with congregations worldwide.
