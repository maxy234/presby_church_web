Frequently Asked Questions

What time are your Sunday services?

We have three services every Sunday: 6:30 AM (Traditional), 9:00 AM (Contemporary), and 11:30 AM (Youth Service).

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a large parking lot behind the church building with security personnel on duty during services.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes, we have Sunday School during the 9:00 AM service and special children's events throughout the month.

How can I become a member?

We offer new member classes on the first Saturday of each month. Contact the church office to register.
